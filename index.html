UniHub brings together the most powerful tools for students, designed with a clean interface that keeps you in flow.

Pomodoro Focus Timer
Customisable Pomodoro sessions with short and long breaks, progress rings, and session streaks to keep you locked in.
GPA Calculator
Calculate semester GPA, save records across all levels and semesters, and track your cumulative performance over time.
Task Manager
Assign tasks to subjects with custom tags, track completion progress, and auto-save everything to your browser.
Progress Dashboard
Visual dashboards showing your study trends, GPA history, and task completion. Identify strengths and areas for improvement.
Focus Music & Sounds

Save and organise your study links, notes, and reference materials in one place. Access everything quickly.
